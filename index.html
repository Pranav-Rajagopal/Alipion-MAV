How SNP helped build The Morphine, a student‑designed fuel‑based rocket engine. This engine was developed through relentless effort by Vishwajeeth V and supported by Adikeshav S of XI Topaz. Shree Niketan Patasala, West Tambaram, provided critical resources, mentorship and technical backing. High‑concentration propellants (80%–99.9%) were handled under strict school‑mandated safety protocols. The engine integrates 3D‑printed SS304L, Teflon, A2212 BLDC motors and an MPU‑6050 IMU. Concepts span CBSE physics and chemistry from Classes 9–11: Newton’s Laws, Thermodynamics, Thermochemistry, Combustion and Projectile Motion. AI assists rocket landing and stabilization. The SNP school management verified all references and enabled expo participation including CBSE Science Expo.
